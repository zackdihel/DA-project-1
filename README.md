# DA-project-1
ECON 5200 Midterm Project
Link to paper "Schooling and Labor Market Consequences of School Construction in Indonesia: Evidence from an Unusual Policy Experiment" by Esther Duflo
https://www.isid.ac.in/~tridip/Teaching/DevelopmentMicroeconomics/Spring2012/Readings/04Education/02Duflo-AER2001.pdf

Data source is a table of survey results of Indonesian men born between 1950 and 1972 (survey conducted in 1995), cross-referenced with data linking adult's education and wages to district-level new school construction between 1973 and 1979 in the respondent's region of birth.
